========================== CYCLONE =============================
		   -------Banan2288-------

                       For 2-4 players
		    Cooperation Required

You've been hired to investigate the Secret Corporation complex
and discover it's true purpose. Massive amount of unknown energy
has been detected and tracked by Wolrd Defending Institution.

If energy have anything common with Alien Colapse, the world is 
in true danger- corporation may attempt to open new portals
to foreign worlds. The problem is those portals can be destroyed 
only from inside, and any lifeform can successfully travel to 
our planet by creating micro-portals around the facility- this
power cannot be controlled.

Additional info: 
Destroy three enemy comunicators to access special weapons.
